Privacy Policy

Premier Order Booking ("we," "our," or "us") is dedicated to safeguarding your privacy when you use our mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, utilize, disclose, and protect your information in the context of our order booking services.

Information We Collect:

User-Provided Information: Premier Order Booking collects information that users provide while using our services. This includes personal details such as names, contact information, and product preferences as entered by salesmen during the order booking process. Transaction Information: We may collect details of the products ordered, order quantities, and transaction history to facilitate order processing and fulfill customer requirements. Geolocation Information: Premier Order Booking may collect and utilize location information to optimize order routing and provide accurate delivery estimates.

How We Use Your Information:

Order Processing: The primary use of collected information is to facilitate the seamless processing of orders between salesmen and shopkeepers. Communication: We may use your contact information to communicate order confirmations, updates, and other relevant information. App Improvement: Analyzing user data allows us to enhance the functionality and features of Premier Order Booking, ensuring an improved and efficient user experience.

Sharing of Your Information:

Shopkeepers and Salesmen: Premier Order Booking facilitates information sharing between shopkeepers and salesmen to enable the efficient booking and fulfillment of orders. Service Providers: We may share information with third-party service providers who assist us in delivering our services, such as order processing and delivery logistics.

Data Security:

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

Your Choices:

Premier Order Booking provides users with certain privacy settings within the App, offering control over the information shared. However, some features may require specific information for optimal functionality.

Updates to This Privacy Policy:

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Users are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy regularly.

Contact Us:

For questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy or this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@premiergroup.com.pk. By using Premier Order Booking, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with these terms, please refrain from using Premier Order Booking.
